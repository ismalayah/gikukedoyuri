Jessica Campbell Biography – Jessica Campbell Wiki

Jessica Campbell was an American actress and naturopathic practitioner. Campbell made appearances in the film Election, in 1999 playing the roles of Tammy Metzler. The appearance made her nominated for an Independent Spirit Award for Best Debut Performance. She also appeared as Julie Gold in the 2001 film The Safety of Objects, and the two-episode guest role of Amy Andrews in the TV series Freaks and Geeks.

Campbell later served as a naturopathic practitioner. In January 2021, Her family announced that she had been found dead on her bathroom floor on December 29, 2020. Her cousin Sarah Wessling, said that before Campbell’s death, she had spent the day attending to patients at her practice, where she served as a naturopathic physician before she returned home to see her family.

Campbell was born on October 30, 1982, in Tulsa, Oklahoma, United States, and grew up in St. Louis, Missouri, United States. She attended Groves High School in St. Louis, Missouri, while there she was a member of the color guard in high school. Campbell went on to Study anthropology in California.
