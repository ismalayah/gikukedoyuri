Who Is Alison Louder? Details On The Actress

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Alison Louder is a Canadian actress. She is known for her roles as Sister Amy on Helix and Emily Levison on Being Human, both for SyFy.

She stars in the award-winning pivotal short film Soft Spot (2017) from the 9-part feature Samanthology (2019) and appears in The Death and Life of John F. Donovan(2018).

She is an alum of Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theater in NYC and the Professional Theatre Department at Dawson College (Dome Theater) in her hometown Montreal.

She also trained with the late Jacqueline McClintock from 2008 until she died in 2012.

Her strong theatre background includes developing several new plays at Playwrights’ Workshop Montreal and Canada’s National Theatre School and going behind the scene as Assistant Director/Producer for Montreal’s premiere production of Ariel Dorfman’s Speak Truth To Power, which sold out and received critical acclaim in 2009.
